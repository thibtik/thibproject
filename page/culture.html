Festival In Cambodia

Happy water festival days

The Cambodian Water Festival, also known as Bon Om Touk, is a three day festival celebrated on the full moon in November and marks a reversal of the flow of the Tonle Sap River.

Traditional Costume

Traditional Cambodian Wedding Ceremony

A traditional Cambodian wedding is comprised of many ceremonies, music, meals, gifts, and guests lasting for three days and three nights

Happy Pchum Ben Festival

It is an important religious festival in the Khmer religious calendar. On this day, Cambodians generally visit pagodas to dedicate food and offerings to those who passed away.
